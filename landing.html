The builder

Not a company.
A student with a problem.

Abdul Wasiq
Python Backend Developer

I'm a Software Engineering student. I built Wysraq because I watched a teacher get buried in WhatsApp messages every weekend — same questions, over and over, from students who couldn't wait.

Every AI solution I found either replied to everything (including your family) or cost money most small businesses can't afford. So I built one that doesn't.

Wysraq runs on FastAPI + PostgreSQL + Node.js + whatsapp-web.js, deployed on DigitalOcean Singapore. The AI is Llama 3.3 70B via Groq. The whole thing handles multiple users, isolated sessions, and real-time WhatsApp messaging — for free.
